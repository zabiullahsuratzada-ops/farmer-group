Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our agricultural divisions and services

What agricultural services does Farmer Group provide in Pakistan?

In Pakistan, Farmer Group offers comprehensive agricultural services including certified seed distribution, modern farming techniques, crop management, soil testing, irrigation solutions, and market linkage services. We serve farmers across Punjab, Sindh, KPK, and Balochistan with region-specific solutions tailored to local conditions and crop requirements.

How can Farmer Group help with horticulture development in Afghanistan?

For Afghan farmers, we provide specialized horticulture services including high-value fruit cultivation, greenhouse establishment, post-harvest management, and export market development. Our expertise helps Afghan farmers maximize returns from horticultural crops in challenging climatic conditions, with particular focus on fruits like pomegranates, grapes, and almonds that have high market potential.

What livestock services does Farmer Group offer in Syria?

In Syria, we provide complete livestock solutions including quality animal supply, veterinary services, feed management, breeding programs, and dairy development. Our services help Syrian farmers rebuild and modernize their livestock operations for sustainable production, with special programs focused on restoring livestock populations and improving animal health in post-conflict regions.

Do you offer consultancy for new farming projects?

Yes, Farmer Group provides comprehensive agricultural consultancy services including farm planning, feasibility studies, project management, technical training, and business development. We support both new and existing farming operations across Pakistan, Afghanistan, and Syria, helping farmers make informed decisions, access modern technologies, and develop sustainable agricultural enterprises.
