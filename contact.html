Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting Farmer Group

How can I contact Farmer Group for agricultural advice?

You can contact Farmer Group through multiple channels: call our regional offices in Pakistan, Afghanistan, or Syria; fill out the contact form on our website; send us an email; or message us on WhatsApp. Our agricultural experts are available to provide personalized advice for your farming needs.

What information should I provide when contacting Farmer Group?

When contacting us, please provide your name, location, contact information, type of farming you're involved in, specific challenges you're facing, and what kind of assistance you need (seeds, livestock, consultancy, etc.). This helps our experts provide the most relevant advice for your situation.

Do you offer free consultations for farmers?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations for all farmers in Pakistan, Afghanistan, and Syria. Our team will assess your needs and provide basic guidance. For more comprehensive farm planning and consultancy services, we offer detailed paid consultations tailored to your specific requirements.

How quickly can I expect a response from Farmer Group?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our regional offices directly or using our WhatsApp business number for faster response. During peak farming seasons, response times may be slightly longer, but we prioritize all farmer inquiries.
